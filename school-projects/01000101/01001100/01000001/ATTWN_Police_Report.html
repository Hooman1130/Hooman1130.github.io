Official Police Report

Devon Police Department

Detective: Henry Grace

Suspect of Interest:
Dr Edward Armstrong

Physical Appearance & Facts:
Dr Edward Armstrong is a fairly athletic middle aged successful Physician. He always has a tense look on his face and is highly skilled in his field of expertise.

Criminal History/ Prior Affiliation:
By the gramophone recording he was accused of causing the death of Louisa Mary Clees.
“Edward George Armstrong, that you did upon the 14th day of March, 1925, cause the death of Louisa Mary Clees.”
He is also somehow connected to the case of Edward Seton, as he was able to recall details from it.
