KATSU Problems Guide

Here are the solutions to almost all of the problems people run into with KATSU.

 

Problems & Solutions

Vostfree doesn't work:
Some animes are down. Nothing we can do about it.

4anime doesn't work:
Update the module in the modules library. There are also instructions that pop-up every time the app is opened.

GoGoAnime/the favorites don't work:
GogoAnime Module has changed its domain, so Orion updated the module. You can find the update on the Modules library. HOWEVER since they changed the domain your favorites from the GoGoAnime module will not work, but Orion has made a tool so you can fix them.

  1. Update the Gogoanime Module.
  2. Go to https://katsu.pw/fix.html and follow the steps, it takes less than 2 minutes and everything will work as always

Video Tutorials

Most of the modules are blocked/don't work:
Try a VPN or AnimePahe.

Don't have a module selected:
Select a module.

The video doesn’t load after I enter the video player:
Choose a different video resolver/server or module.

When I select a video resolver/server, nothing happens:
Select a different video resolver/server or module.

Something weird is going on with the Android version I got from the Play Store (or got from anywhere but https://katsu.pw):
The one you downloaded was an imposter trying to steal your data. The official version is an apk installed from https://katsu.pw. A useful video on how to install APKS can be found here.

The downloads aren't working:
When downloading videos here are some things to remember.

  • Don’t download videos from main page, search for them instead.
  • Not every module and resolver works for downloads.
  • If the download finishes instantly from 0-100% that means it didn’t work.
  • Sometimes the video may be down, so Katsu can’t download something that’s not there
  • You may have no tickets.

I have a different issue:
That is highly unlikely. If you truly do have a different issue, please explain it in the KATSU discord server. When explaining the issue, please always first state which module you are using. Any new issues found will be added to this guide.
